About Srinivasa Ramanujan

Srinivasa Ramanujan was one of the greatest mathematicians in history. He is celebrated for his deep intuition in number theory, infinite series, partitions, continued fractions, and remarkable mathematical identities.

One of the most famous mathematical curiosities associated with him is the Ramanujan magic square, often linked to his birth date: 22 December 1887.

Inspired by that beautiful idea, this project creates a personalized birthday-based 4×4 magic square using a user’s birth date and also supports reverse search, PNG export, and shareable links.

Why this project matters

This project combines mathematics, creativity, and web development into a simple but memorable user experience. It allows users to generate a personalized Ramanujan-style birthday magic square, verify it, export it as PNG, and share it.

It also serves as a portfolio-quality example of a modern static web app with PWA support, SEO optimization, analytics, share links, and a polished UI.
